Request Account Deletion – NSCDC Response App

This page allows verified responders using the NSCDC Response App to request permanent deletion of their account and associated data.

What Will Be Deleted

Once processed, we will delete the following information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Assigned role and organization (e.g., NSCDC, private security)
  • Login history

How to Request Deletion

Email our support team with your registered information:

Email: support@safecity.ng

Account Deletion Request – NSCDC App

Include your registered phone number and role (e.g., NSCDC official) to verify your identity.

Retention Policy

Data will be deleted within 7 business days unless retention is required for auditing or legal reasons.

Need help? Visit the Help section within the app or contact support.
